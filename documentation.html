Welcome to NutriChoice!

About Us

NutriChoice is a small creative website which aims at giving the users a easy look up of recipes and cooking ideas. It is designed to serve as a one stop choice for cooking enthusiast by providing diverse recipes along with its nutrition information. We help users to stay healthy by finding recipes that match the target calorie intake. The variety of options presented in the user’s search catalogue makes it possible to find recipes for what’s in the fridge, nutritional requirements, favorite ingredients, cuisines and more.

Search Features

  • Search by Ingredients - Enter the required Ingredients(separated by comma if there is more than one) and click the find recipe button below
  • Search by Nutrients - Enter the nutrienst Information(in ierms of calorie count/max carbs/fat/ protein in the corresponding text boxes provided) and click the find recipe button below.
  • Random Search - Select one or more of the prefered cusine/meals from the options listed and click the find recipe button below to get all recipes that match the selected option.
  • based on the search options sepecified the results of search will be displayed below. The user can click on any of the displayed options to view the details further.

Recipe Features

  • The recipePage displays all the details of the selected recipe such as the its corresponding set of instructions and data such as cooking time, ingredients, nutritional facts etc.
  • In case of unavailabilty of any ingredient the user can click the "alt?" link available next to each ingredient to check for alternative Ingredients
  • Apart from these a list of similar recipes are listed below
